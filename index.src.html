How to Play

Connect two artists through song features.

You're given a Start and a Target artist. Your job is to build a chain between them by finding artists they've both appeared on songs with.

Example
Saba → "Ultralight Beam" → Kanye West → "Forever" → Drake → "Chicago Freestyle" → Giveon
  • Click + to add an artist or song in the chain
  • Use Artist or Song mode to search
  • The rail lights up green when you connect two artists
  • Easy uses the top 250 most famous artists
  • Hard expands to the top 500
  • Daily gives everyone the same puzzle each day — no hints!
